MF2 – 1025 | Grand Opening of the Drunken Moon

The Drunken Moon opened its awning for the first time beneath a restless sky. With us we hauled an unwieldy white behemoth of a tent — one that creaked and bowed as the wind tested its patience.

Our patronage was astonishing. The night was long, the benches were filled, musicians and dancers carried the revel, and a hearty fire burned at the heart of the bar. All were welcome. All were well watered.

By the end of the night our coffers were heavy with coin.

Yet as the hour grew late, a thief barreled through our patrons and made off with the till — a weighty mix of coin summing many legends.

Through keen persistence by the Drunken Moon crew, aided by Nation Support and Guild Leaders, the coin was recovered and the matter resolved with haste.

— Set down by the Bar
