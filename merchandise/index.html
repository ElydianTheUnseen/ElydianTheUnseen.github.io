Gulpers

“If it gulps, it will guard it.”

Gulpers are said to be stitched with an appetite of their own, forever loyal to the bearer who feeds them stories and oddments. Enchanted cloth bags for the travelling adventurer. Excellent for bog witches!

Beyond the tale: Gulpers are hand-made and designed by Elydian, crafted for durability and character, each with its own quirks. If you cannot find the perfect companion then maybe they can be crafted.

Seek them beneath crimson banners.

×

Pinchshrooms

“Grown beneath laughter and thread.”

Pinchshrooms are coaxed into being slowly, shaped by needle and thread then brought to life with just a touch of enchantment. These little coin-keepers make the perfect travel companion. Their grasping hands are ready to hitch a ride on a satchel strap, a belt or even your own hair!

Beyond the tale: Each Pinchshroom is hand-made and designed by Elydian, never rushed, never identical.

If one finds you, it was meant to.

×

Shams Distillations

“Filtered through myth. Bottled in moonlight.”

Shams Distillations are said to be brewed from leftover courage, questionable decisions, and whatever was on hand at the time. Beware! Some of these goods are products of The Hedge - forgotten brews now found by the infamous Sham and peddled for a fair price.

Beyond the tale: These props and curios are hand-crafted by Sham to bring tavern lore into the waking world. Most contents are drinkable so please advise Sham if you have allergies.

Not all spirits are meant to be consumed.

Seek the crimson banners. Follow the sound of drums.
