The Drunken Moon

A Valdraethii Tavern of the Court of the Crimson Moon

Beneath crimson banners and a watchful moon, the Drunken Moon stands as a refuge for denizens, wanderers, and schemers alike.

Born of Valdraethii hands and bound to the Court of the Crimson Moon, we are not merely a tavern — we are a gathering place, a cultural hub, and a reliable source of questionable decisions.

Affordable Booze

No extortionate prices. No noble mark-ups. Just honest drink poured heavy and often.

Ample Seating

Benches, tables, corners to brood in — space enough for conspiracies, camaraderie, and collapse.

Drums & Antics

Home to the finest drummer this side of the Menhir, accompanied by song, chaos, and ill-advised dares.

Seek the crimson banners. Follow the sound of drums. The Moon is already watching.
